Race for the Earth 5k

Sat October 21, 2020

Boom Island, MN US 55414

Our Cause

Our mission with this 5k is to do our part in the fight against global warming and climate catastrophe

By signing-up to run or donating, you are taking part in this fight alongside us.

Place & Time

Start Line: Boom Island Park, Minneapolis MN

Finish Line: Boom Island Park, Minneapolis MN

Time: TBD

Description

This race will take you on a scenic stroll through some of Minneapolis' greatest landmarks.

During the run, you will run through West River Park as the leaves change, cross partway over the Hennepin Bridge to historic Nicollette Island and finally loop back to Boom Island for the finish and a wonderful view of the skyline.
